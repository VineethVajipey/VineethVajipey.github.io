My mind was burnt, but my energy was spiky and agitated. I've made a lot of progress since then. I have a much more amicable relationship with the bee now. My awareness seems to have been multiplied, given the new framework I am trying out. Of course, it's based in trust not perception, I've given absolute trust from my end, but it doesn't really matter. Life is good, even in the bad moments. Life is an opportunity, I definitely won't waste it.

Focus completely on the process. 3. Give yourself completely, without expectation. Consistency is the key. I want to quickly blast away at my projects with complete abandon.
